Cookie policy

How BANUB uses cookies and local browser storage.

Last updated: 18 June 2026

This policy explains how BANUB LTD uses cookies and similar technologies on this website.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. Similar technologies include local storage, session storage, pixels and software development tools. They can remember preferences, keep products in a shopping cart and help a website operate securely.

2. Technologies currently used

The current version of this website uses local browser storage for essential functions such as:

  • remembering products and quantities added to the shopping cart;
  • storing order-request references on the device;
  • remembering a local account email where you choose to use that feature;
  • remembering newsletter and cookie preferences;
  • supporting basic website functionality.

These functions are necessary to provide features you request. Clearing browser storage may remove your cart, saved references and preferences.

3. Categories of cookies

  • Strictly necessary: required for core functions such as security, cart operation and preference storage.
  • Functional: remember choices and improve convenience.
  • Analytics: help understand how visitors use the website. These should not be activated without the appropriate consent where consent is required.
  • Advertising: used to personalise or measure advertising. These should not be activated without the appropriate consent where consent is required.

4. Third-party technologies

If we introduce third-party services such as analytics, embedded media, payment tools, customer support or advertising, those providers may place their own cookies. This policy and the on-site consent controls will be updated to identify those services before they are used.

5. Your choices

You can accept or reject optional cookies through any cookie controls presented on the website. You can also delete or block cookies and local storage through your browser settings. Blocking necessary storage may prevent parts of the website from working properly.

6. Legal basis

Strictly necessary technologies are used because they are needed to provide the website or a feature requested by you. Optional technologies are used only on an appropriate lawful basis and, where required by the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations, after consent.

7. Changes

We may update this policy when technologies or service providers change. The latest version will be published here.

8. Contact

Questions about cookies can be submitted through our contact page.
