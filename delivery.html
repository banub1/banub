Delivery information

How delivery timing, charges and order updates are handled.

Last updated: 18 June 2026

1. Order-request process

The website currently allows customers to submit an order request. After receiving your request, we may contact you to confirm product availability, delivery options, delivery charges and payment arrangements. Submitting a request does not guarantee acceptance or dispatch.

2. Delivery areas

Available delivery areas depend on the product, destination and courier. United Kingdom delivery is the primary service area. International delivery may be available for selected products and will be confirmed before the order is accepted.

3. Delivery charges

Delivery charges depend on destination, parcel size, weight and service level. Any applicable charge will be confirmed before the order is accepted. A promotional free-delivery threshold applies only where stated and subject to the relevant conditions.

4. Delivery estimates

Estimated dispatch and delivery dates will be provided where available. Estimates are not guaranteed unless expressly agreed. Delivery may take longer during public holidays, severe weather, high-demand periods or circumstances outside our reasonable control.

5. Accurate address information

You are responsible for providing a complete and accurate address, postcode, country and any necessary access instructions. Contact us promptly if you notice an error. We cannot guarantee that an address can be changed after preparation or dispatch has begun.

6. Receiving the delivery

Some deliveries may require a signature, age verification or attendance at the address. If delivery cannot be completed, the courier may leave instructions, attempt redelivery or return the parcel. Additional charges caused by repeated failed delivery or incorrect information may be payable.

7. Damage or missing items

Inspect the parcel as soon as reasonably possible. Contact us promptly if an item is damaged, missing or incorrect and include the order reference and photographs where appropriate.

8. Delays and events outside our control

If delivery is materially delayed, we will provide information where reasonably possible. Your legal rights relating to late delivery are not affected.

9. Risk

Risk in the goods passes to you when they are delivered to you or a person identified by you, subject to applicable consumer law.

10. Contact

Questions about delivery can be submitted through our contact page. Include your order reference where available.
