Returns & refunds

Our policy for cancellations, returns, faulty goods and refunds.

Last updated: 18 June 2026

We want you to be satisfied with your purchase. This policy applies to consumer purchases from BANUB LTD and is in addition to your statutory rights.

1. Voluntary 30-day return policy

For most products, we offer a voluntary return period of 30 days from the date the goods are delivered. To qualify, the item should be unused, in a resalable condition and, where reasonably possible, returned with its original packaging, accessories and documentation.

To start a return, contact us before sending anything back. We will confirm the correct return instructions and return address. Our registered office is not automatically the returns address.

2. Statutory cancellation right

For most online purchases, UK consumers have a legal right to cancel within 14 days beginning the day after the goods are received. After notifying us of cancellation, you normally have a further 14 days to return the goods.

Where an entire order is cancelled under the statutory right, we will also refund the standard outbound delivery charge. Any premium delivery upgrade is refundable only up to the cost of standard delivery.

3. How to request a return

Use our contact form and include your email address, order reference, product name, quantity and reason for return. We will respond with the next steps and, where applicable, a return authorisation or label.

4. Return postage

Unless an item is faulty, damaged, incorrect or not as described, you are responsible for the cost and safe return of the item. We recommend using a tracked service and keeping proof of postage. We may refuse a refund where a return is not received and no satisfactory evidence of posting is available.

5. Items that cannot usually be returned

Change-of-mind returns may not be available for:

  • personalised, bespoke or made-to-order products;
  • perishable goods;
  • sealed hygiene-sensitive goods once unsealed;
  • sealed audio, video or software products once opened;
  • digital content once supply has begun with the required consent;
  • gift cards or vouchers, except where required by law;
  • goods that have been mixed inseparably with other items after delivery.

6. Faulty, damaged or incorrect goods

Please contact us as soon as possible if an item is faulty, damaged, incorrect or not as described. We may ask for photographs or other reasonable information so we can assess the issue.

Under the Consumer Rights Act 2015, goods must be of satisfactory quality, fit for purpose and as described. Depending on the circumstances, you may be entitled to a repair, replacement, price reduction or refund. Your statutory rights are not affected by this policy.

7. Condition of returned goods

You may inspect goods in the same way you reasonably could in a shop. We may reduce a refund to reflect any loss in value caused by handling beyond what is necessary to establish the nature, characteristics and functioning of the goods.

8. Refund method and timing

Approved refunds will normally be made to the original payment method. We will process a refund without undue delay and, where the statutory cancellation rules apply, generally within 14 days after receiving the returned goods or receiving satisfactory evidence that they were sent back, whichever is earlier.

Your bank or payment provider may take additional time to display the refund.

9. Cancelling before dispatch

Contact us promptly if you wish to cancel before dispatch. We will try to stop the order, but this may not be possible once preparation or delivery has started.

10. Exchanges

Where an exchange is available, we will confirm the process. If an exchange is not available, you may need to return the original item for a refund and place a new order.

11. Contact

Return and refund requests should be submitted through our contact page. Please do not send goods to the registered office unless we specifically confirm that address for your return.
